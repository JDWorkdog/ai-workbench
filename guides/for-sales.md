As a sales professional, you'll get the most value from:
1. **Research** - Prospect research, industry analysis, competitive intel
2. **Communication** - Email outreach, follow-ups, proposals
3. **Presentations** - Deck creation, style consistency
4. **Preparation** - Meeting prep, objection handling

## Tips for Sales

### Prospect Research
- Research the company AND the person
- Look for recent news, funding, initiatives
- Identify pain points relevant to your solution
- Find common ground for personalization

### Email Outreach
- Personalize based on research
- Keep it concise - respect their time
- Clear call to action
- Request multiple variations to test

### Proposal Customization
- Extract prospect's brand colors and fonts
- Match their style in your materials
- Show you understand their business

### Meeting Preparation
- Brief on company background
- Prepare for likely objections
- Have relevant case studies ready
- Know the competitive landscape

## Example Workflows

**Pre-Call Research:**
1. Use Research prompt with prospect company
2. Ask for recent news, key initiatives, challenges
3. Identify talking points and personalization angles
4. Review before your call

**Custom Proposal:**
1. Extract style from prospect's website or deck
2. Apply their brand colors to your proposal
3. Personalize content based on research
4. Stand out from generic proposals

**Email Sequence:**
1. Use prompt engineering to build outreach prompts
2. Generate multiple email variations
3. A/B test subject lines and approaches
4. Iterate based on results

## Integration Tips

### With Your CRM
- Use research outputs to enrich contact records
- Save effective prompts for team use
- Track which approaches work best

### With Presentation Tools
- Export style guidelines to design tools
- Create branded templates
- Maintain consistency across materials
