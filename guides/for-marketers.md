# AI Workbench for Marketers

Your guide to AI-powered marketing workflows.

## Quick Start

As a marketer, you'll get the most value from:
1. **Research** - Market trends, competitive analysis, audience insights
2. **Content Creation** - Blog posts, social media, email campaigns
3. **Brainstorming** - Campaign ideas, messaging, positioning
4. **Style Extraction** - Analyze brand guidelines from existing assets

## Tips for Marketers

### Research Workflows
- Specify your industry and target audience
- Ask for data sources and citations
- Request analysis of trends over time
- Compare against specific competitors

### Content Creation
- Provide brand voice guidelines
- Include examples of content you like
- Specify word counts and formats
- Request multiple variations

### Brand Consistency
- Upload your style guide to Claude Projects
- Use style extraction to document existing assets
- Reference brand guidelines in your prompts

## Example Workflows

**Competitive Research:**
1. Use the Research prompt
2. Specify competitors and topics
3. Request a comparative analysis
4. Ask for actionable insights

**Content from Brand Assets:**
1. Extract style from existing presentations
2. Use those guidelines in content prompts
3. Maintain brand consistency automatically
